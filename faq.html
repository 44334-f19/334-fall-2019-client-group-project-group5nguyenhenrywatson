FAQ


What is a Parti Yorkie?

See our About Yorkies page for more information on the differences between a regular Yorkie and a Parti Yorkie!

How old are your puppies?

Our puppies vary in age (see the individual information on the Puppies page to find their ages), but they typically are 8+ weeks old before we are able to send them home with you. This way, we can guarantee that the puppy has been properly nursed by the mother and is able to eat on its own at the point of transaction.

Feel free to visit our Contact page and call or email us about this, or any other questions you may have. We are more than willing to put your mind at rest by providing proof that we are fully invested in the welfare of our puppies and we strive to meet AKC standards that guarantee you will be getting what you paid for!
