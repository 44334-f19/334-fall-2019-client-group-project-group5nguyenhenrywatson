ABOUT

Wanting to learn more about Parti Yorkies? Maybe you don't know what exactly a Parti Yorkie is, or what distinguishes it from what you see as a 'standard' Yorkie. Or, maybe you just flat out don't know what a Yorkie is either. No judgement! We have the perfect page for you: About Parti Yorkies. This page contains credible sources and AKC-affiliated websites that will teach you just what our type of pup is, how we do our part, and how to raise a Yorkie pup.

On the other hand, maybe you wanted to learn more about the Skistimas family and get to know the people who care for these precious pups. We've also got that for you too: About the Family. This page has a fun little introduction to the family and gives you more insight into the family's background in caring for animals of various shapes and sizes.
